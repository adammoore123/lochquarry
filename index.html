Lochquarry Outdoor Centre is set in acres of land in the heart of the majestic Argyll hills. On our doorstep is not only magnificent scenery, but also a breath-taking selection of outdoor and adventurous activities. With activities designed to meet the needs
of all ages and experiences of young people, Lochquarry truly brings adventure to everyone. Each activity is
run under the instruction of one of our highly trained staff and all safety equipment is provided.


Our activities are very popular with youth groups including Scouts and Guides.
